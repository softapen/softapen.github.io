Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some common questions about our services and process.

What is your typical process for new projects?

We begin each project with an initial consultation to understand your needs. Then we provide a proposal outlining scope, timeline, and cost. Once approved, we move into design and development phases with regular check-ins before final delivery and launch.

How long does it typically take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary based on your scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 3-6 months. We’ll provide a detailed timeline once we understand your requirements.

Do you offer maintenance services after project completion?

Yes! We offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and running smoothly. These can include regular updates, security monitoring, content changes, and technical support.

What information do you need to get started?

To get started, we typically need your business requirements, project goals, target audience, brand assets (logo, colors, etc.), content, and any specific features or functionality you need.

Do you offer rush services for urgent projects?

Yes, we can accommodate rush projects depending on our current workload. Rush services may include additional fees, which we’ll discuss during our consultation.
